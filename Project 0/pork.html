Stewed pork ham with vegetables

Ingredients

  • 1.5 kg piece of ham
  • 3-4 red onions
  • 3 large garlic cloves
  • 2 big carrots
  • 1 yellow pepper
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1 organic chicken stock cube
  • 1 glass of red wine
  • 7 black pepper corns
  • 5 dried cloves
  • 2 bay leaves
  • ½ a bunch of fresh parsley, optional
  • olive oil
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Method

  1. Peel the onions, garlic and carrots and deseed the peppers.
  2. Place a large pan on a medium-high heat and fry the ham with a splash of oil until golden, turning occasionally with tongs. Remove the ham to a baking dish, leaving the pan on the heat.
  3. Cut onions, carrots, peppers into a large cubes. Chop the garlic. Add onions and garlic to the pan to cook for a few minutes. Add peppers and let it cook down for around 5 minutes.
  4. Add sauted vegetables to a baking dish.
  5. In mug of boiling water dissolve chicken stock cube. Add the broth and the wine to a baking dish.
  6. Add some salt and pepper to taste, also add black pepper corns, cloves and bay leaves to a dish.
  7. Cover a baking dish with foil. Turn the oven for 150°C and put a baking dish there for 1.5 hours.
  8. Remove the foil carefully and let it cook down for around 1.5 hours
  9. Serve the ham with fresh chopped parsley and mashed potatoes.
